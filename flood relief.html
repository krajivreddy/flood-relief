Major Floods in India

An overview of significant floods that impacted India

2014 Jammu and Kashmir Floods

In September 2014, heavy rainfall led to massive flooding in Jammu and Kashmir, affecting over 2 million people.

2008 Bihar Floods

The Kosi River breached its embankment in August 2008, displacing millions and causing extensive damage in Bihar.

2015 Chennai Floods

In November-December 2015, Chennai faced one of its worst floods in a century, leading to significant loss of life and property.
